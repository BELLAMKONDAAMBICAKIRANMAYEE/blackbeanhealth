INTRODUCTION TO BLACKBEAN HEALTH

THE CRUCIAL EDGE THAT YOUR CLINICAL PRACTICE NEEDS

Blackbean Health is driven by the commitment to provide Doctors with the best lifestyle and access to external world-class facilities that make medical practice trouble-free, rewarding, and successful. In keeping with its mission, Blackbean is delighted to offer Blackbean Health Edge – an innovative programme designed by experts to empower Doctors to practise with ease. The programme spans the unique combination of Hospital-as-a Service, Marketing-as-a-Service, and CRM-as-a-Service that enables Doctors to build their own identity, boost their clinical practice, and manage patients efficiently.

THE CARE THAT YOUR PATIENTS DESERVE

The feature rich programme is just as beneficial to patients. It serves as an end-to-end solution to patients for all their healthcare needs. It builds and nurtures the bond between a doctor and their patients by personalising care, automating interactions, digitising health records, and through information dissemination using innovative distribution channels. It democratises health care and elevates patient experience to new levels.
